Careers at Crezalo

Crezalo is a fast-growing creator monetization platform that empowers creators to monetize their content and connect with their fans. We're passionate about building a platform that makes it easy for creators to sell access to their premium videos, courses, and merchandise.

We're looking for talented individuals to join our team and help us build the next generation of creator monetization platform. If you're passionate about building products that make a difference, we'd love to hear from you.

Current Openings:

1. Frontend Engineer

As a frontend engineer, you will be responsible for building and maintaining our frontend applications using React and React Native. You will work closely with our product and design teams to implement new features and ensure the best user experience for our creators and fans.

Requirements:

1. 2+ years of experience in frontend development using React and React Native

2. Experience with Redux, CSS, and HTML

3. Good understanding of web and mobile application architecture

4. Familiarity with RESTful APIs

2. Backend Engineer

As a backend engineer, you will be responsible for building and maintaining our backend services using Node.js and AWS. You will work closely with our frontend and product teams to ensure the best user experience for our creators and fans.

Requirements:

1. 2+ years of experience in backend development using Node.js

2. Experience with AWS services such as EC2, Lambda, S3, and RDS

3. Good understanding of web application architecture and RESTful APIs

4. Familiarity with SQL and NoSQL databases

3. UI/UX Designer

As a UI/UX designer, you will be responsible for designing and implementing the user interface and user experience for our web and mobile applications. You will work closely with our product and frontend teams to ensure the best user experience for our creators and fans.

Requirements:

1. 2+ years of experience in UI/UX design for web and mobile applications

2. Proficiency in design tools such as Sketch, Figma, and Adobe Creative Suite

3. Good understanding of user-centered design principles and design thinking methodologies

4. Strong communication and collaboration skills

4. Content Marketer

As a content marketer, you will be responsible for creating and executing content marketing strategies that drive user acquisition and engagement. You will work closely with our product and marketing teams to ensure the best user experience for our creators and fans.

Requirements:

1. 2+ years of experience in content marketing for web and mobile applications

2. Experience in creating content for blogs, social media, and email marketing

3. Good understanding of SEO, SEM, and social media marketing

4. Strong communication and collaboration skills

If you're interested in any of these positions, please send your resume and portfolio to careers@crezalo.com. We look forward to hearing from you!
